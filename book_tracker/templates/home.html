Welcome to BookTracker!

This app was created to help you track your reading progress. You know those times when you think 'Oh, I should definitely make a mental note to read this book' and a few hours later this note has mysteriously disappeared? Yeah, we can help you with that. And also with tracking progress of your current reading list (which can be a great motivation not to get stuck on that particularly boring chapter of academic book you're reading).
You're welcome to add the books you've already read to your 'Have Read' bookshelf and share your opinions on them with others by writing reviews. Let people know what they're getting themselves into!
So hurry up, click 'Browse Books' on your navigation bar and get started!
